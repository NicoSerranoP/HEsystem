1. Wallet Tutorial

To buy things on the Internet without intermediaries or thrid-parties involve you need a virtual wallet. Our system recevies payments on Ether, the second most famous cryptocurrency in the world. This tutorial describe each step to create a virtual wallet and get Ether.

1. Install Metamask

The most easy and secure form of managing a virtual wallet is by the Metamask browser extension (available for Chrome, Brave, Firefox, Edge). Download the extension in the official website and install it in your browser.

2. Create a virtual wallet

By clicking on the Metamask icon in the right superior corner of your browser, you would get the required steps to create a new wallet (password, backup words, etc).

3. Buy Ether

Metamask gives you the direct option to buy Ether with the Wyre and CoinSwitch companies. Buy the amount you consider you would need (the remaining would always be stored in your wallet).

4. Get your credentials

Go to the Account Options / Account Details section in the superior right corner next to your account name (Account 1 by default). Your account address is the visible string of characters starting with 0x... and the string of characters that would appear after clicking "Export Private Key" is your private key (something like your password so be careful with it).

Optional: Use the testnet

ONLY FOR DEVELOPERS: If you are configuring the purchase system and need to do some tests, you can use the testnet "Ropsten" by selecting it in the superior right corner tab for networks (Mainnet by default). To fill your wallet with test Ether go to  https://faucet.metamask.io/.
